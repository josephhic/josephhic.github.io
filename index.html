Introduction

I am a DPhil (PhD) candidate at the University of Oxford in the Materials Department studying under Dr Natalia Ares and Professor Andrew Briggs. My research focuses on improving the scalability of quantum devices using machine learning. I am funded by the EPSRC through a DTP studentship.
